FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What are the requirements to get these documents?

To check the specific requirements for obtaining documents, please visit our Requirements Page for detailed information.

Who can book appointments?

All students, both undergraduate and graduate, as well as TUP Manila alumni, are eligible to book appointments through the TUP AppointMate system.

What's the process for obtaining a document through this appointment system?

The process is simple and efficient.

STEP 1:

BOOK APPOINTMENT

Navigate to the TUP AppointMate platform.
Select your desired service and book an appointment at your convenience.
Provide necessary information, including the purpose of the appointment

STEP 2:

ADMIN APPROVAL

Wait for the Registrar to review and approve your appointment request.
You'll receive a notification once your appointment is approved.

STEP 3:

DOCUMENT RETRIEVAL

On your scheduled date and time, visit the Registrar's Office to pay and claim your documents hassle-free.

Can I change or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your appointment by logging into your TUP AppointMate account. Navigate to the "Appointments" section, where you'll find options to adjust or cancel your scheduled appointments.

What happens if I miss my appointment?

If you miss your scheduled appointment, you will be rescheduled at the last slot of your chosen date. Please note that missed appointments may result in delays in document processing.

Is my information secure on TUP AppointMate?

Yes, you can request multiple documents in a single appointment. Simply choose the documents you want to request when filling up the appointment information.

Can I request for multiple documents in one appointment?

Yes, you can request multiple documents in a single appointment. Simply choose the documents you want to request when filling up the appointment information.

Are there additional fees for using TUP AppointMate?

No, using TUP AppointMate is free of charge. However, please be aware of any specific fees associated with the document request process.
